Welcome to my little corner of the universe!
I'm finishing my Master's at PUC Chile. My research focuses on Emission Line Galaxies (ELGs).
I study Hα, [OIII], and [OII] emitters using data from the LAGER and CIDER surveys to understand how galaxies evolve across cosmic time.
