About Me

Welcome! I am a Master's student in Astrophysics at Pontificia Universidad Católica de Chile. My research interests focus on galaxy evolution and star formation in the early universe.

Currently, I am working on my thesis, "Tracing Star Formation with Hα, [OIII], and [OII] Emitters in LAGER Fields." I use deep imaging data from DECam to measure the Star Formation Rate Density (SFRD) at different epochs.

I specialize in using Python for astronomical data analysis (Astropy, Photutils) and source extraction tools like SExtractor.
