M.Sc. Thesis

Tracing Star Formation with Emitters in LAGER Fields

2024 - Present | Supervisors: F. Barrientos & P. Hibon

I am analyzing 15 deg² from the LAGER survey using CTIO/Blanco DECam imaging. By performing photometric redshift estimation and SED fitting, I identify Hα, [OIII], and [OII] emitters to construct luminosity functions and trace cosmic star formation evolution.

Bachelor's Thesis

Luminosity Function of Galaxies in LAGER

2023 | PUC & ESO

Analyzed 3 deg² of the NSF-1 field. Identified emission-line galaxies via narrow-band selection at z=0.47, 0.93, and 1.59. This work laid the foundation for my current Master's thesis by establishing the selection pipeline.

Course Project

Cluster Galaxies in Abell 2744

2023 | Supervisor: F. Bauer

Performed a panchromatic study of cluster galaxies using NUV-NIR photometry. I used CIGALE for SED fitting to derive stellar masses, ages, and star formation rates to study galaxy evolution in dense environments.
