My work on Galaxy Evolution

Tracing Star Formation with H&alpha, [OIII], and [OII] Emitters from the LAGER Survey

2024 - Present | M.Sc. Thesis

I'm analyzing 15 deg² of DECam imaging from the LAGER survey. The goal is to identify emission-line galaxies via narrow-band selection at z = 0.47 (H&alpha), z = 0.93 ([OIII]), and z = 1.59 ([OII]). I constructed luminosity functions across all fields to trace the evolution of star formation over cosmic time.

Luminosity Function of Galaxies with Emission Lines in LAGER Fields.”

2023 | Bachelor's Thesis

Analyzed 3 deg² of the NSF-1 field. I built a pipeline to select emission-line galaxies via narrow-band selection at z=0.47, 0.93, and 1.59. I obtained redshifts with LePHARE and Bagpipes to classify galaxies, and computed the luminosity function at each redshift.
